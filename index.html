The Gibson County Animal Shelter is an important part of the community in Gibson County. We provide temporary homes for animals in need. We provide options for the animals in need to get adopted into permanent homes. We have decicated staff and a dedictaed team of volunteers ready to help with anything at any time. We are available at the shelter from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. and you can reach us at (731)-855-0771. You can visit our Facebook page to learn more about us here. Call us if you ever see an animal that looks like it needs help. We will be there to pick it up as soon as possible. Also, don't forget to stop by and adopt a pet for you or a loved one!

The Gibson County Animal Shelter is locazted at 1252B Manufacturer's Row, Trenton, Tennessee 38382.
